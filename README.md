## What is Deli Dash?

Deli Dash is a browser deli game where you serve a consecutive stream of hungry customers before their patience runs out. Build the right sandwich, serve it in time, and keep the line moving.

---

## How to Play

1. **Open the game** via the GitHub Pages link above 
2. **Read the order bubble** above each customer's head 
3. **Click the ingredients** on the counter to build your sandwich on the plate
4. **Hit SERVE** when your sandwich matches the order
5. **Watch the patience bar** on the right side of each customer if it hits 0, they walk out and you lose points
6. **Click the plate** to remove the top ingredient if you make a mistake, or hit **CLEAR** to start fresh
7. **Score as high as you can** before the 120 second timer runs out!

### Tips
- Serve customers in order — the leftmost customer at the counter always gets served first
- The faster you serve, the more bonus points you earn from leftover patience
- Orders get longer as your level increases, so stay sharp

## The Theme: Consecutive

Everything about Deli Dash is **consecutive**.

The gameplay is built around a consecutive stream of customers, and never stop coming until time is up. Your job is to keep up with the chain.

The soundtrack leans into this too. The background music is composed around **parallel octaves** — also known as **consecutive octaves** in music theory, where both voices/parts play in two octaves simultaneously, one right after the other. I am by no means a music theory expert so this may not be entirely right.
